BPM Playlister

A personal tool for generating BPM-based music playlists from YouTube Music libraries. Perfect for:

  • Running & Jogging (120-140 BPM)
  • Cycling & Cardio (140-160 BPM)
  • Walking & Warm-up (90-120 BPM)
  • High-Intensity Training (160+ BPM)

Features:

  • Automatic BPM detection and matching
  • Smart playlist generation for different activities
  • YouTube Music integration
  • Custom BPM range support

BPM Data Source:

This application uses comprehensive BPM (Beats Per Minute) data powered by GetSongBPM.com

GetSongBPM.com provides accurate tempo information for millions of songs, enabling precise playlist curation for fitness and activity-specific music needs.
